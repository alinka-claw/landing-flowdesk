FAQ

Got questions?

Can I import data from other tools?

Yes! We support one-click imports from Trello, Asana, Notion, Jira, and Monday. Your boards, tasks, and comments come with you.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Absolutely. Every new account gets a 14-day Pro trial automatically. No credit card required.

What happens when my trial ends?

You'll be moved to the Free plan. No data is lost — you just lose access to Pro features until you upgrade.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or startups?

Yes — 50% off Pro for verified nonprofits and startups under 2 years old. Reach out to our sales team.

How do I cancel?

One click in Settings. No dark patterns, no retention emails, no guilt trips. We respect your choice.
